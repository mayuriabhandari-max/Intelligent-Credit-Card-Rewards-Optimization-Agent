ISSUER: HDFC Bank

EFFECTIVE DATE: 2026-01-01

REWARD PROGRAM OVERVIEW
HDFC Diners Club Black earns "Reward Points" (RP) on eligible spends. Reward Points can be redeemed as
cashback, against travel bookings, or transferred to airline and hotel partners at variable ratios.

CATEGORY-WISE REWARD STRUCTURE
1. Flights: Standard rate of 3.33 Reward Points per Rs. 150 spent (approx 2.2 points per Rs. 100). No
   additional acceleration for flights specifically.
2. Hotels: When booked through the SmartBuy portal, accelerated rate of 5 Reward Points per Rs. 150 spent.
   When booked directly with a hotel, standard rate applies (2.2 points per Rs. 100).
3. Dining: Accelerated rate of 5x, i.e. approximately 11 Reward Points per Rs. 150 spent, when paid via the
   partnered dining program. Outside the program, standard rate applies.
4. Groceries: Standard rate of 3.33 Reward Points per Rs. 150.
5. Utilities: Standard rate applies, no exclusion, but capped (see caps section).
6. Rent payments: EXCLUDED entirely on all rent payment platforms.
7. Insurance premiums: Standard rate applies, no exclusion, capped at 5,000 Reward Points per statement cycle
   for insurance category specifically.
8. Wallet reloads: EXCLUDED entirely.

CAPS
- Utilities category is capped at 2,000 Reward Points per statement cycle; any spend beyond this cap in the
  utilities category earns zero additional points.
- Insurance category capped at 5,000 Reward Points per statement cycle.
- No cap on flights, hotel (SmartBuy), dining (partnered) or grocery categories.

MILESTONE BENEFITS
- On reaching Rs. 4,00,000 annual spend, cardholder receives a complimentary international lounge access
  set (8 visits).
- On reaching Rs. 8,00,000 annual spend, cardholder receives 10,000 bonus Reward Points.

REDEMPTION OPTIONS
- Redeem as cashback (statement credit) at Rs. 0.30 per Reward Point (lower value redemption).
- Redeem against flight/hotel bookings on SmartBuy at approximately Rs. 0.50 per Reward Point.
- Transfer Reward Points to airline and hotel partners at variable ratios (see below). This is typically the
  highest-value redemption option if done during favourable periods.

TRANSFER PARTNERS
- Partner: Air Voyage Miles (airline) | Type: airline | Transfer ratio: 3 Reward Points = 1 Air Voyage Mile |
  Minimum transfer: 3,000 Reward Points | Maximum transfer: 150,000 Reward Points per year.
- Partner: GlobalStay Hotel Points (hotel) | Type: hotel | Transfer ratio: 3 Reward Points = 1 GlobalStay Point |
  Minimum transfer: 3,000 Reward Points | Maximum transfer: 120,000 Reward Points per year.

ANNUAL FEE
- Annual fee: Rs. 10,000 + taxes. Waived on annual spend above Rs. 5,00,000 in the previous year.
