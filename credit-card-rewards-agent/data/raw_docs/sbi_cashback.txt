CARD NAME: SBI Cashback
ISSUER: State Bank of India
DOCUMENT TYPE: Reward Program Terms
EFFECTIVE DATE: 2026-01-01

REWARD PROGRAM OVERVIEW
SBI Cashback does not use a points system. It gives direct cashback as a percentage of eligible spend,
credited automatically to the statement.

CATEGORY-WISE REWARD STRUCTURE
1. Online spends (general, including flights and hotels booked online): 5% cashback.
2. Offline / in-store spends (general): 1% cashback.
3. Dining: 1% cashback (treated as offline unless ordered via an online food delivery app, in which case the
   5% online rate applies).
4. Groceries (online): 5% cashback. Groceries (offline / in-store): 1% cashback.
5. Utilities: EXCLUDED from the 5% online cashback rate. Utilities bill payments earn only 1% regardless of
   channel.
6. Rent payments: EXCLUDED entirely. No cashback earned on rent payment transactions.
7. Insurance premium payments: EXCLUDED entirely. No cashback earned on insurance premium payments.
8. Wallet reloads and gift cards: EXCLUDED entirely.
9. Fuel: EXCLUDED entirely (standard surcharge waiver only, no cashback).

CAPS
- Maximum cashback of Rs. 5,000 per statement cycle across all categories combined.
- Beyond the Rs. 5,000 cap, no further cashback is earned for the remainder of that billing cycle.

MILESTONE BENEFITS
- No milestone benefits are offered on this card.

REDEMPTION OPTIONS
- Cashback is automatically credited to the statement; no manual redemption or point transfer is required
  or possible. This card does not support transfers to airline or hotel partners.

TRANSFER PARTNERS
- Not applicable. SBI Cashback has no transfer partner program.

ANNUAL FEE
- Annual fee: Rs. 999 + taxes. Waived on annual spend above Rs. 2,00,000 in the previous year.
