CARD NAME: Axis Atlas
ISSUER: Axis Bank
DOCUMENT TYPE: Reward Program Terms
EFFECTIVE DATE: 2026-01-01

REWARD PROGRAM OVERVIEW
Axis Atlas earns "Edge Miles" on eligible retail spends. Edge Miles are the base reward currency for this
card and can be redeemed against travel bookings or transferred to airline and hotel partners.

CATEGORY-WISE REWARD STRUCTURE
1. Flights (booked via any travel portal or airline directly): Accelerated rate of 5 Edge Miles per Rs. 100 spent.
2. Hotels (booked via any channel): Accelerated rate of 5 Edge Miles per Rs. 100 spent.
3. Dining: Standard rate of 2 Edge Miles per Rs. 100 spent.
4. Groceries: Standard rate of 2 Edge Miles per Rs. 100 spent.
5. Utilities (electricity, water, gas bill payments): EXCLUDED from accelerated rewards. Only 1 Edge Mile per
   Rs. 200 spent (heavily reduced rate).
6. Rent payments: EXCLUDED entirely. No Edge Miles are earned on rent payment transactions processed
   through any rent payment platform.
7. Insurance premium payments: EXCLUDED from accelerated rewards. Standard rate of 1 Edge Mile per Rs. 200.
8. Wallet reloads and gift card purchases: EXCLUDED entirely.
9. International spends: Accelerated rate of 5 Edge Miles per Rs. 100, same as flights/hotels.

CAPS
- Monthly cap on accelerated categories (flights + hotels combined): maximum 20,000 Edge Miles per
  statement cycle. Any spend beyond the cap earns Edge Miles at the standard rate of 2 per Rs. 100.
- No cap on standard-rate categories.

MILESTONE BENEFITS
- On reaching Rs. 3,00,000 annual spend, cardholder receives 2,500 bonus Edge Miles.
- On reaching Rs. 7,50,000 annual spend, cardholder receives an additional 2,500 bonus Edge Miles plus a
  complimentary domestic lounge access voucher.

REDEMPTION OPTIONS
- Redeem Edge Miles directly against flight and hotel bookings on the Axis Travel Edge portal at a fixed
  value of Rs. 1 per Edge Mile.
- Transfer Edge Miles to airline and hotel loyalty partners (see transfer partner table below).

TRANSFER PARTNERS
- Partner: Air Voyage Miles (airline) | Type: airline | Transfer ratio: 1 Edge Mile = 1 Air Voyage Mile |
  Minimum transfer: 1,000 Edge Miles | Maximum transfer: 200,000 Edge Miles per year.
- Partner: GlobalStay Hotel Points (hotel) | Type: hotel | Transfer ratio: 2 Edge Miles = 1 GlobalStay Point |
  Minimum transfer: 2,000 Edge Miles | Maximum transfer: 100,000 Edge Miles per year.

ANNUAL FEE
- Annual fee: Rs. 5,000 + taxes. Waived if annual spend in the previous year exceeds Rs. 4,00,000.
